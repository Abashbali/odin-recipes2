Abhishekbhai Zuchiini

Zuchiini

Description

Made with Bisquick,this zucchini is delicious,easy,and perfect for any occasions zucchini,cheddar cheese,and onions are baked together,resulting in irresistible bite sized appetizers.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Sliced Zucchini
  • 1 cup all purpose baking mix
  • 4 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic,minced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees c)
  2. Mix Zucchini,all purpose baking mix,eggs,onion,cheddar,oil,garlic,and salt together in a large bowl until well combined.Spread mixture into the prepared baking dish
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and lightly browned,about 25minutes.Cut into 36 pieces
